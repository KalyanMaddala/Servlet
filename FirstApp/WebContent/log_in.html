How do I log into my Facebook account?
To log into your Facebook account on a computer:

Go to facebook.com. 
At the top under Email or Phone, enter one of the following:

Email:
You can log in with any email that's listed on your Facebook account.

Phone number: 
If you have a mobile number confirmed on your account, you can enter it here (don't add any zeros before the country code, or any symbols).

Username: 
You can also log in with your username, if you set one up.

Under Password,
enter your password.

Click Log In.
